About BlameStella

I'm Delano. I built Stella to scratch an itch. That is, a web monitoring service that met my needs as a web developer. I've worked on sites large and small and I'm putting everything I learned into building great software for others.

I launched the service in 2010 and relaunched on January 1st, 2013. I've had a lot of fun and learned a lot along the way. I'm using this blog to write about my experiences running the company and the technology behind it.
